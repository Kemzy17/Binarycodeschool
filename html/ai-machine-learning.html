Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Skill Requirements


As artificial intelligence grows more relevant across all major industries, data and other tech professionals with concentrated AI and machine learning experience are more in-demand than ever before. Specialization in this field may require different skills—depending on your unique professional experience and goals—but successful AI specialists are typically skilled in many of the following concepts and technologies:


  • Data science processes, data wrangling, data exploration, and hypothesis building and testing
  • Applied data science with Python
  • Machine learning models and accuracy metrics
  • Deep Learning concepts and their relevant applications and tools, such as Keras, Python, and TensorFlow
  • Text-to-speech conversion with automated speech recognition
  • Deep Neural Networks

Additionally, those looking to enter an AI and machine learning specialty should possess a combination of knowledge, skills, and professional experience in data analytics or a related data field.
